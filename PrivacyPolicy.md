Welcome to Kugo! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information when you use our platform at https://www.kugo.chat and our related applications and services (together, the "Kugo Services").

We genuinely care about your privacy. We want you to understand how we collect, use, and share your information—and the choices you have. Whether you're connecting with new friends, streaming live, or simply exploring, we're committed to being transparent about our data practices.

When you use Kugo, you may share personal information with others. For example, when you create your profile, broadcast content, or interact with other users—that information becomes part of your social experience. Since Kugo is built around real connections, some of your activity may be visible to other users. We encourage you to be thoughtful about what you share.

---

## 1. What Information Do We Collect

We collect information to provide you with the best possible experience on Kugo. Here's what we gather and how:

### i. Information You Provide to Us

When you sign up, create your profile, or use our features, you choose to share information with us, including:

- **Registration details** — Your date of birth, phone number (or email), password, user ID, and language preference

- **Profile information** — Your display name, profile photos, gender, location, bio, social media links, education, and career details

- **Content you create** — Comments, messages, photos, videos, live streams, and any other content you share on Kugo

- **Facial data** — When you use our special effects or emoji features, we process your facial data locally to create these effects. This data isn't used for any other purpose without your explicit consent. We don't use facial data for marketing or share it with third parties. For profile verification, we compare your photo with a verification video to confirm you're real—we don't store the verification video afterward

- **Payment information** — Bank account, PayPal, or other payment details when you make purchases

- **Transaction records** — Purchase history and transaction IDs for paid services

- **Your contacts** — With your permission, we can access your phone contacts to help you find friends on Kugo

- **Location data** — Your GPS location when you allow it (you can change this in your device settings anytime)

- **Communications with us** — Questions, feedback, or complaints you send to our support team

- **Survey and activity data** — Information you provide when participating in surveys, contests, or promotions

- **Social media connections** — Information from Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, Google, or other platforms when you connect them to your Kugo account

---

### ii. Information We Collect Automatically

When you use Kugo, we automatically collect certain information to keep our services running smoothly and to improve your experience:

- **Activity data** — Your browsing history, search queries, videos you've watched, pages visited, when you were active, and how you interact with other users

- **Information from other sources** — Additional data from games, services, or social networks you've connected to Kugo

- **Device information** — Your operating system, browser type, device model and serial number, IP address, carrier, screen resolution, language settings, and device identifiers (IMEI, IMSI, MAC address)

- **App usage** — Information about how you use other apps on your device

- **Location indicators** — Location information from your SIM card

- **Advertising IDs** — Mobile identifiers (Apple's IDFA or Google's AAID) that help us and our partners show you relevant ads

- **Content metadata** — Information about your uploads like hashtags, captions, and when content was created

- **Cookies and similar tech** — Small data files that help us remember your preferences, understand how you use Kugo, and improve our services

We may also create aggregated, anonymized data from your information—this doesn't identify you personally and helps us understand overall usage patterns.

Our use of cookies is governed by our Cookie Policy.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We only use your information when we have a valid reason. Here's what we do with it:

- **Providing our services** — Showing you content, enabling features, verifying your identity, and delivering prizes for promotions

- **Making things better** — Improving and personalizing Kugo based on your preferences and feedback

- **Managing your account** — Handling your registration, providing support, and sending important notices about your account or our services

- **Staying in touch** — Communicating with you about updates, promotions, and changes to Kugo

- **Keeping content safe** — Reviewing content to ensure it complies with our guidelines and applicable laws

- **Personalizing your experience** — Analyzing your interests to show you content, products, and ads you might like

- **Understanding usage** — Tracking metrics like visitor counts, video views, and demographic patterns

- **Security and safety** — Verifying age requirements, fixing technical issues, and detecting fraud or illegal activity

- **Legal compliance** — Enforcing our terms and meeting our legal obligations

- **Creating insights** — Combining information for the purposes above

### About Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies (like web beacons and pixel tags) to understand how you use Kugo, enhance your experience, and improve our services. By using Kugo, you consent to our use of cookies.

We may link your contact information with your activity across devices. We're not responsible for third-party privacy practices—their policies apply to their services.

You can adjust cookie settings in your browser. Note that disabling cookies may affect some Kugo features, and you may need to adjust settings on each browser and device you use.

## 3. How We Share Your Information

If your profile is public, your content can be seen by anyone on Kugo and may be accessed or shared by others, including search engines and news sites. You can control who sees each video or photo you upload.

We may share your information with our corporate group (parent companies, subsidiaries, affiliates) to improve our platform and prevent misuse.

We share your information when you've given us consent, either directly or by your actions.

**With our corporate family** — We may share data with companies in our group for services, account management, personalization, analytics, security, and compliance.

**With service providers** — Trusted partners who help us with order fulfillment, payment processing, content personalization, analytics, security, data storage, and other features. They can only use your data to provide these services.

**When you connect third-party services** — If you link Kugo with another service, you're authorizing us to share information with them.

**In business transitions** — If we're involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We'll ensure the new entity honors this privacy policy.

**For legal reasons** — We may share information with authorities when required to comply with laws, enforce our terms, investigate violations, protect safety, or maintain our platform's security.

**Aggregated data** — We may share anonymized, combined data with partners for business analysis. This doesn't identify you personally.

## 7. Your Choices

You have control over your information:

- **Cookies** — Adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies. Note that some Kugo features may not work properly without them

- **Push notifications** — Turn these off in your device settings anytime

- **Device permissions** — Disable GPS, microphone, camera, or other features in your device settings. You can also adjust privacy settings within Kugo to hide your location, videos, and activity

- **Limited information** — You can choose not to provide certain information, but this may prevent you from using some features, making purchases, or participating in promotions

- **Advertising preferences** — Change your device's advertising settings or visit http://www.networkadvertising.org/mobile-choice or http://youradchoices.com/appchoices

- **Update your info** — Edit your profile directly in the app to access, correct, or delete your information

## 9. Children Safety

**Kugo is for users 18 years and older.** We don't intentionally collect personal information from minors, though we may collect age data to verify eligibility.

If we discover we've collected information from someone under 18, we'll promptly delete it and ban the account. Please don't use Kugo if you're under 18.

### Reporting Child Sexual Abuse Content (CSAM)

If you encounter potential CSAM on Kugo, please use our in-app report feature immediately. Our support team is available 24/7 and will review reports quickly. Confirmed CSAM will be reported to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC).

You can also report through our official website's "Children Safety" page, which links directly to NCMEC.

## 10. Retention of Your Information

We keep your personal information as long as you maintain your Kugo account.

**To delete your account:**
- Go to: Me > Settings > Account > Delete Account in the app, or
- Email solli.devid@gmail.com

After your request and our review, we'll delete data that isn't needed for legal, regulatory, tax, or insurance purposes. We may retain some information temporarily for safety, security, fraud prevention, and research.

In some cases, we can't immediately delete your account—for example, if there's an outstanding balance or unresolved dispute. We'll delete it once the issue is resolved.

We may also retain limited information about deactivated accounts to prevent abuse.

## 12. How to Delete Your Account

We've made account deletion straightforward:

1. Open the app and go to **Settings > Account Settings > Delete Account**
2. Follow the on-screen instructions

For your security, we'll keep your account for **7 days** after you confirm deletion. During this time, you can log back in to restore everything.

After 7 days, all your data is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

If you've uninstalled the app, you can submit a deletion request through our website.
